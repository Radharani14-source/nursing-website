Peer Evaluator Directory

ACEN Peer Evaluators

An ACEN Peer Evaluator is a volunteer representative of the ACEN who makes recommendations for a
program's accreditation status to the ACEN Board of Commissioners (BOC). Peer evaluators may be nurse
administrators, nurse educators, or nurse clinicians.
